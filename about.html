The Soul Behind
The Experience

Discovery

Elevating Every
Al Fresco Moment

Picnique was founded on a singular principal: that the most profound connections happen when we slow down and appreciate the beauty of our surroundings. What began as a boutique styling service for intimate gatherings has evolved into a full-scale luxury event firm specializing in bespoke outdoor experiences.

Our philosophy is rooted in "Curation over Decoration." We don't just provide props; we design environments that stimulate all five senses. From the scent of hand-picked florals to the texture of hand-woven Turkish rugs and the taste of locally-sourced gourmet delights, every element is an intentional choice.

Today, we represent the gold standard in pop-up styling, bridging the gap between nature's raw beauty and the refined elegance of high-end hospitality.
