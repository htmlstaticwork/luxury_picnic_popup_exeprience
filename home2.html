Your Questions, Answered

Do you provide the food or do we bring our own?

You can choose! We offer premium chef-curated charcuterie and brunch boxes, but you are also welcome to bring your own delicacies. We provide all the beautiful plating and glassware either way.

What happens if it rains on the day of my booking?

Picnic magic shouldn't be ruined by weather. We offer free rescheduling within 48 hours notice, or we can move your pop-up to an indoor location of your choice.
